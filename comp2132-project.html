How to Play

  • To begin, click "Roll Dice" and a pair of dice will be rolled.
  • To proceed to the next round, click "Roll Dice" again.
  • After three consecutive rounds the score is tallied and the game is over.
  • To play again, click on "New Game".

Rules

  • There are three rounds.
  • At the end of each round, the score is tallied based on the sum of the dice.
  • If two of the same numbers are rolled, then the players score is multiplied by two.
  • However, if the outcome of either of the two dice is one, then the score for that round is zero.
  • The player with the most points at the end of the three rounds wins.
